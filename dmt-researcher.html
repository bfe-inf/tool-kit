DATA MANAGEMENT TRAINING INVENTORY

For Researchers

  • Organization: DataONE Education Modules
    Link: https://www.dataone.org/education-modules
    Contains: Lessons on all phases of the research lifecycle involving data. While all of the modules are useful in understanding data management, the following lessons are of particular help to researchers: Lesson 4: Data Entry and Manipulation; Lesson 5: Data Quality Control and Assurance; Lesson 8: Data Citation.
  • Organization: Data Tree by the Institute for Environmental Analytics and the Natural Environment Research Council
    Link: https://datatree.org.uk/
    Contains: Data management training targeted primarily for PhD students and early career researchers in the environmental sciences
  • Organization: MANTRA Research Data Management Training
    Link: https://mantra.edina.ac.uk/careerresearcher.html
    Contains: Data management training specific to professional role; this specific link contains information for career researchers.
