DMT: South America

Data management training resources specific to the continent of South America, including in-person training in South America, virtual resources from South American research organizations, and data management guides written specifically for South American researchers

  • Location: Colombia
  • Organization: International Center for Tropical Agriculture - CIAT
    Topics: Resources to help researchers produce high quality, reusable and open data from research activities. These resources, in the form of documents, templates, and videos, are organized by the role of the individual (PI, Researcher, or Data Manager), the stage of the research project, and by category, such as managing data, metadata, archiving & sharing, etc.
    Format: Virtual resources
    Link: https://ciat.cgiar.org/data-management-support-pack/
  • Location: Worldwide (Varies)
    Organization: Committee on Data of the International Council for Science - Research Data Alliance (CODATA-RDA)
    Topics: Various; foundational data skills across disciplines
    Format: In-person workshops
    Link: http://www.codata.org/events/training-workshops
