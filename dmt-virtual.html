DATA MANAGEMENT TRAINING INVENTORY

Virtual Training

  • Organization: Australian Data Research Commons
    Link: https://www.ands.org.au/workingwith-data/skills/23-research-data-things
    Contains: 23 Research Data Things, a resource for managing data
  • Organization: CMMI Institute
    Link: https://cmmiinstitute.com/learning/training/upcoming
    Contains: Enterprise data management training and certification
  • Organization: Consortium of European Social Science Data Archives (CESSDA), European Research Infrastructure Consortium
    Link: https://www.cessda.eu/Research-Infrastructure/Training
    Contains: Training on research data management in the social sciences
  • Organization: Data Observation Network for Earth (DataONE)
    Link: https://www.dataone.org/
    Contains: Resources supporting the discovery of Earth and environmental data, and the proper management of said data
  • Organization: Data Tree by the Institute for Environmental Analytics and the Natural Environment Research Council
    Link: https://datatree.org.uk/
    Contains: Data management training targeted primarily for PhD students and early career researchers in the environmental sciences
  • Organization: Digital Preservation Coalition (DPC)
    Link: https://www.dpconline.org/
    Contains: Digital preservation webinars and resources
  • Organization: ESIP Data Management Training Clearinghouse
    Link: http://dmtclearinghouse.esipfed.org/
    Contains: A registry for online learning resources focusing on research data management
  • Organization: European Space Agency (ESA)
    Link: http://www.esa.int/Education/ESA_training_material
    Contains: Resources and materials from workshops for students and data users
  • Organization: Federation of Earth Science Information Partners (ESIP Federation)
    Link: http://commons.esipfed.org/datamanagementshortcourse
    Contains: Data Management Short Course for Scientists, in partnership with NOAA and the Data Conservancy
  • Organization: Fostering the Practical Implementation of Open Science in Horizon 2020 and Beyond (FOSTER Plus)
    Link: https://www.fosteropenscience.eu/
    Contains: Links to data management courses, training materials on open science, text and data mining, research data management, and responsible research and innovation
  • Organization: International Association for Social Science Information Services and Technology (IASSIST)
    Link: http://www.iassistdata.org/resources
    Contains: Professional resources from IASSIST members on data management and data literacy
  • Organization: MANTRA at The University of Edinburgh
    Link: https://mantra.edina.ac.uk/
    Contains: Modules and resources in data management training specific to role (research student, career researcher, senior academic, and information professional)
  • Organization: Maximising the Exploitation of Linked Open Data in Enterprise and Science (MELODIES) Project
    Link: http://www.melodiesproject.eu
    Contains: Environmental data and visualizations
  • Organization: MetEd
    Link: https://www.meted.ucar.edu/index.php
    Contains: Teaching and training resources for the geoscience community
  • Organization: Oak Ridge National Laboratory Distributed Active Archive Center for Biogeochemical Dynamics (ORNL DAAC)
    Link: https://daac.ornl.gov/resources/
    Contains: Webinar links, tutorials, and code examples for preparing a data management plan and properly formatting data for long term-archive
  • Organization: Research Data Alliance
    Link: https://www.rd-alliance.org/events/training-webinars
    Contains: Links to future and past data management training and webinars
  • Organization: RRI Tools
    Link: https://www.rri-tools.eu/training/about
    Contains: Information on data training sessions in Europe and training modules
  • Organization: UK Data Service
    Link: https://www.ukdataservice.ac.uk/
    Contains: Data repositories, data skill modules, and information on upcoming webinars
