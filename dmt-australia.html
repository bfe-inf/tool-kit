DMT: Australia

Data management training resources specific to the continent of Australia, including in-person training in Australia, virtual resources from Australian research organizations, and data management guides written specifically for Australian researchers

  • Location: Across Australia (Adelaide, Brisbane, Canberra, Melbourne, Parramatta, Perth, Sydney)
    Organization: Data Management Association (DAMA)
    Topics: Resources for data management trainings, in-person events, and guides. DAMA also offers a mentoring program which connects individuals to Australia's most senior and prominent data professionals from various industries, as well as teaching and administering the international certification program - Certified Data Mangement Professional (CDMP).
    Format: In-person and virtual trainings; online guides and physical guides for purchase
    Link: https://www.dama.org.au/index.php
  • Location: Melbourne and Canberra
    Organization: Australian National Data Service - ANDS
    Topics: ANDS provides a guide for best practices that accesses data management across 5 levels of maturity: initial, development, defined, managed, and optimised. They provide policy checklists, data management plans, as well as resources to identify areas to enhance and extend data management capability.
    Format: Virtual resources
    Links: https://www.ands.org.au/online-services/rif-cs-schema
    https://www.ands.org.au/guides
    https://www.ands.org.au/guides/creating-a-data-management-framework
    https://www.ands.org.au/working-with-data/data-management
