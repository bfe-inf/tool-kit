DMT: Europe

Data management training resources specific to the continent of Europe, including in-person training in Europe, virtual resources from European research organizations, and data management guides written specifically for European researchers

  • Location: Worldwide (Varies)
    Organization: Committee on Data of the International Council for Science - Research Data Alliance (CODATA-RDA)
    Topics: Various; foundational data skills across disciplines
    Format: In-person workshops
    Link: http://www.codata.org/events/training-workshops
  • Location: Alpbach, Austria
    Organization: Summer School Alpbach
    Topics: Space science and engineering
    Format: In-person summer school workshop
    Link: https://www.summerschoolalpbach.at/
  • Location: United Kingdom
    Organization: Centre for Environmental Data Analysis (CEDA)
    Topics: Big data storage, processing, and collaboration; scientific computing
    Format: In-person workshops with some archived training material
    Link: http://www.ceda.ac.uk/training/past-courses/
  • Location: Finland
    Organization: Centre for Scientific Computing
    Topics: Fortran 95/2003 and C programming; high-performance computing tools; parallel programming
    Format: In-person summer school and online webinars
    Link: https://www.csc.fi/en/web/training/archive
  • Location: United Kingdom
    Organization: Scientific Exploration of Operational Missions - European Space Agency (SEOM-ESA)
    Topics: Cryosphere studies; train next generation of Earth Observation scientist to exploit data from ESA
    Format: In-person course in 2016
    Link: http://seom.esa.int/cryotraining2016/
  • Location: Various, primarily United Kingdom
    Organization: Software Sustainability Institute
    Topics: High-performance computing, software and data carpentry
    Format: In-person workshops
    Link: https://www.software.ac.uk/what-do-we-do/training
  • Location: United Kingdom and online
    Organization: UK Data Service
    Topics: Data best practices, data management plans, open data sharing
    Format: In-person workshops, internet modules, and printed advice
    Link: https://www.ukdataservice.ac.uk/manage-data/training
  • Location: Essex, United Kingdom
    Organization: University of Essex Summer School
    Topics: Social Science and Data Analysis
    Format: In-person summer school
    Link: http://essexsummerschool.com/summer-school-facts/
  • Location: Essex, United Kingdom
    Organization: University of Essex Summer School
    Topics: Big Data and Analytics
    Format: In-person summer school
    Link: https://www.essex.ac.uk/research/institutes-and-centres/institute-for-analytics-and-data-science/big-data-and-analytics-summer-school
  • Location: Oxford, United Kingdom
    Organization: University of Oxford Summer School
    Topics: Digital humanities-- text processing, linked data, data management
    Format: In-person summer school
    Link: https://digital.humanities.ox.ac.uk/dhoxss/
