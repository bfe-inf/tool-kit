DMT: Professional Certifications

A professional certification in data management is a credential that signifies an advanced understanding of data management principles and practice. Most certification programs are fee-based and generally offered on a specific schedule:

  • Organization: Data Management Association International (DAMA I)
    Certification: Certified Data Management Professional (CDMP)
    Link: https://dama.org/content/cdmp-status
  • Organization: Microsoft
    Certification: Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert in Data Management and Analytics (MCSE)
    Link: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/learning/mcse-data-management-analytics.aspx
