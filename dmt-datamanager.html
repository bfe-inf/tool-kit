DMT for Data Managers

While all members of a research team should have an understanding of proper data management, data managers are expected to provide leadership on creating and enforcing an effective data management plan, formulate techniques for quality data collection, and implement measures for data security and longevity. For a list of applicable training and certifications, see the resources below:

  • Organization: Data Management Association International (DAMA I)
    Certification: Certified Data Management Professional (CDMP)
    Link: https://dama.org/content/cdmp-status
  • Organization: DataONE Education Modules
    Link: https://www.dataone.org/education-modules
    Contains: Lessons on all phases of the research lifecycle involving data. While all of the modules are useful in understanding data management, the following lessons are of particular help to data managers: Lesson 1: Why Data Management; Lesson 3: Data Management Planning; Lesson 6: Protecting Your Data; Lesson 10: Legal and Policy Issues.

  • Organization: MANTRA Research Data Management Training
    Link: https://mantra.edina.ac.uk/informationprofessional.html
    Contains: Data management training specific to professional role; this specific link contains resources for information professionals (including data managers).

  • Organization: Microsoft
    Certification: Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert in Data Management and Analytics (MCSE)
    Link: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/learning/mcse-data-management-analytics.aspx
